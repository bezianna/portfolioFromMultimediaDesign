User Experience through understanding

"It is amazing how often people solve the problem before them without bothering to question it. Engineers and businesspeople are trained to solve problems. Designers are trained to discover the real problems. A brilliant solution to the wrong problem can be worse than no solution at all: solve the correct problem."

/Don Norman, The Design of Everyday Things
This is the principle of User Experience design. To start every project by determining the problem and continue with research, design thinking in teams, then developing a wonderful product based on constant iterations including the refinement after testing, so the best possible UX can be achieved.
This is how I am also educated at UCN, Aalborg, currently enrolled in my third semester.

Selected Works

#Slovakfolklore

Slovakfolklore's online presence was confined to social media. Based on user research, it was discovered, that a website would help the company's credibility with providing necessary information in one platform.

The website was developed in collaboration with the company and with two other students. The focus was on prototyping and reaching the best possible UX.

Guldsmed Juvelerer Apel

This jewellery site is a complete redesign of Guldsmed Jevelerer Apel's existing webpage. The product is made by a two-member group and the development process included qualitative research, competitor research & analysis and information architecture.

The main focus is on the user interface design and the visual representation of the jewellery store's elegant and luxurious identity.

Pain(th)ing

Pain(th)ing is a platform holding art contests focusing on both traditional and contemporary art, including digital paintings It is developed in collaboration with Ludwig Muzeum.

The group focused on UX and UI research and on the function to upload art-works to a realtime database and to showcase them.
